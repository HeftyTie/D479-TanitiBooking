Discover Taniti, a small tropical paradise in the Pacific. Despite it's modest size of less than 500 square miles, Taniti boasts a diverse landscape that is sure to captivate everyone's heart!.

Picture yourself strolling along the pristine sandy and rocky beaches, to fishing on our small but safe harbor, or perhaps take on the adventure of exploring the lush tropical rainforests and take a marvel at looking at the breathaking mountainous interior, location of Mount Ire, a small active volcano.

At the heart of Taniti lies an indigenous community, extending warm greetings to visitors like you. With a population of around 20,000, the island exudes a friendly and welcoming atmosphere. Until recently, Taniti thrived on its rich fishing and agricultural traditions, and now, with a growing interest in tourism, we're excited to share our vibrant culture and natural wonders with you.
