Transportation to the Island

Almost all our wonderful visitors visit the island by air, ensuring a breathtaking aerial view of our paradise! While others arrive on a charming small cruise ship that docks in the picturesque Yellow Leaf Bay once a week.

Taniti's small but bustling airport is well-equipped for your convenience, ready to welcome small jets and propeller planes. And here's the exciting part—Taniti is currently in the process of expanding the airport!

Transporation in the Island

Explore with Ease! Taniti has your ground transportation covered for a seamless and upbeat island adventure! Public buses make traversing Taniti City a breeze, operating daily from 5 a.m. to 11 p.m.

For those eager to explore the rest of our stunning island, private buses are at your service, ensuring you don't miss a single corner of Taniti's beauty. Need a quicker ride? Taxis in Taniti City are ready to whisk you away to your destination, offering a dash of convenience and a dash of excitement!

Planning to go off the beaten path? Rent a car from the local agency near the airport, and let the open road be your playground. Feeling more eco-friendly? Embrace the island breeze on rented bikes, complete with required helmets for safety and style. Taniti City, with its flat terrain, is a paradise for walking enthusiasts.

And speaking of walking, many happy tourists choose to stay around Merriton Landing – an area so easy to explore on foot that you'll find every step reveals a new delightful discovery. Get ready to dance your way through Taniti, where every mode of transportation promises an upbeat adventure!
